# Positioning
---

## Core Proposition

> I bring design, product and business thinking together to turn fragmented ideas into visions teams can actually act on.

**With subheadline:**
> Progress happens when everyone involved aligns around concepts they can see and believe in. My job is to create that clarity.

**Shorter:**
> I help companies turn fragmented ideas into concepts their teams can execute.

---

## The Problem I Solve

Clients have fragmented ideas and perspectives scattered across departments (product, design, business, marketing). They can't move forward because there's no shared, tangible vision to align around. The actual issue is often disguised as something else: "We need a product redesign," "We need a better pitch," "We need to align on priorities."

**The transformation:** From "we've been trying to do this for months/years" to "it's actually happening."

---

## Three Engagement Modes

### 1. Vision Sprint (2–12 weeks)
Intensive focus to synthesise fragmented ideas into a clear, tangible concept.

**For:** Early-stage visions or fragments with no clear direction yet.

**Delivers:** Vision artefacts (journey maps, experience flows, IA frameworks, vision decks) that teams can test, validate, and build from.

### 2. Embedded Design Leadership (3–12 months)
Leadership and stewardship from concept through delivery — orchestrating teams, unblocking progress, ensuring vision translates into execution.

**For:** Complex initiatives requiring ongoing facilitation across functions, stakeholder management, and design decisions at scale.

**Delivers:** Outcomes (shipped features, validated experiments, cross-team alignment) + vision artefacts that guide execution.

### 3. Strategic Clarity (project-based / ongoing)
Designing and facilitating the co-creation process to clarify and communicate strategic direction — for a brand, a product, or an ecosystem of products.

**For:** Organisations going through change that need everyone aligned on where they're headed — product pivots, re-envisioning a product or channel, foreseeing how an ecosystem of products could develop.

**Delivers:** Shared understanding of direction + a compelling narrative artefact that sustains alignment and commitment.

## Differentiators

**Core differentiator:** I move fluidly across altitudes — from framing the problem and defining outcomes, to making it tangible through visions, narratives, and prototypes teams can build from. It's not sequential: making things tangible is how the framing sharpens. Most people operate at one altitude. I cover the full range, and I bring people along through the process. Underlying this is the ability to synthesise across domains (product, business, user needs, tech constraints) and translate that synthesis into tangible artefacts that unlock alignment.

**Draft differentiator line:**
> AI makes building easy — and easily scattered. Direction is scarce. I zoom out to frame it, zoom in to make it tangible. The craft is in doing both, and bringing everyone along.

**Supporting lines:**
1. **I speak product and business** — and design the concepts that bring them together.
2. **I make things visible.** That changes how people see the problem — and unlocks shared understanding.
3. **I shape what doesn't exist yet** into something people can react to and build from.

## Who Hires Me

**Primary:** Design leaders (VP, Head of, Lead) at growth-stage companies who recognise multi-stakeholder complexity and see design as strategic, not just execution.

**Secondary:** CPOs who understand this is strategy work, not design outsourcing. Founding teams needing external thinking to clarify early-stage vision.

---

## How I Work

- **Not just user-centred** — I bring everyone to the table. People commit to a vision when it serves their interests.
- **Validate only what matters** — I make assumptions, reuse existing insights, and focus validation on what truly moves things forward.
- **Always a recommendation** — No matter how complex the situation, I provide a clear next step.
