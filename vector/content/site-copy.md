**Illustration:** Hand-drawn. Represents collaboration and synthesis. Sits above the reactive statement, static.

**Cards (accordion — one open at a time):**

### Vision Sprint — 2–12 weeks
You have fragments of an idea but no shared direction yet. I synthesise research, stakeholder perspectives, and strategic context into tangible artefacts — journey maps, experience flows, IA frameworks, vision decks — that your team can test, validate, and build from.

### Embedded Design Leadership — 3–12 months
A complex initiative needs ongoing orchestration across functions — from concept through delivery. I embed with your team, orchestrate stakeholder alignment, drive design decisions, and keep vision and execution connected.

### Strategic Clarity — Project-based
Your organisation is going through change and needs everyone pointed in the same direction. I design and facilitate the process that turns scattered perspectives into a shared narrative — something that sustains alignment after the engagement ends.

**Differentiator line (below accordion):**
Strategy consultants align on direction — but deliver abstractions. Design agencies execute — but need direction handed to them. I work in the middle: I align through tangible design.
